Lasagna!

This Lasagna is one of the best lasagna's you will ever try.

It's a simple yet delicious recipe.

Ingredients!

  1. Meat
  2. Onion and Garlic
  3. Tomato Products
  4. Sugar
  5. Lasagna Noodles
  6. Cheeses
  7. Egg

Steps and Instructions!

  1. Gather all your ingrediants
  2. Cook Meats
  3. Stir in crushed tomates
  4. Boil large pot of water
  5. Combine ricotta cheese with egg
  6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F
  7. Assemble
  8. Layer
  9. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes
  10. Rest
  11. ENJOY!
